HumanProx

Get set up in under 2 minutes. Install two things, hit Start, and join your interview.

1
Install BlackHole
BlackHole is a free audio driver that lets HumanProx hear your interview audio. Download BlackHole 2ch and run the installer.
After installing, open Audio MIDI Setup (search in Spotlight).
Click the + button at the bottom left and select Create Multi-Output Device.
Check both your speakers/headphones AND BlackHole 2ch.
Set this Multi-Output Device as your Mac's sound output in System Settings → Sound.
2
Install HumanProx
Download the app, open the .dmg file, and drag HumanProx to your Applications folder.
Download for Mac
If macOS says the app is from an unidentified developer:
Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security → scroll down and click Open Anyway.
3
Start
Open HumanProx. Enter your name, make sure BlackHole 2ch is selected as System Audio, pick your microphone, and hit Start.
Then open your interview meeting (Zoom, Google Meet, etc.) and start the interview normally. HumanProx runs quietly in the background.

That's it. Your coach will see your session automatically. Just focus on the interview.
