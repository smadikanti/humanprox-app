HumanProx

Get set up in under 5 minutes. You'll need your invite code, BlackHole installed, and the HumanProx app.

1
Install BlackHole
BlackHole is a free audio driver that lets HumanProx hear your interview audio. Download BlackHole 2ch and run the installer.
After installing, open Audio MIDI Setup (search in Spotlight).

Click the + button at the bottom left → Create Multi-Output Device.

In the right panel, check two boxes:
  • Your normal speakers or headphones (e.g. MacBook Pro Speakers)
  • BlackHole 2ch

Then go to System Settings → Sound → Output and select the Multi-Output Device you just created. You'll still hear audio normally.
2
Install HumanProx
Download the app, open the .dmg file, and drag HumanProx to your Applications folder.
Download for Mac
macOS will block the first launch. This is normal.

Open Terminal (search in Spotlight) and paste this command:
xattr -cr /Applications/HumanProx.app Press Enter. Then open HumanProx from Applications — it will launch normally.

If you see "Apple could not verify" instead, click Done, then go to System Settings → Privacy & Security and click Open Anyway.
3
Fill in Your Interview Details
Open HumanProx. You'll see these fields to fill in:
Invite Code — the 6-character code your coach shared with you. Required.

Your Name — so your coach can identify your session.

Audio Devices — select BlackHole 2ch as System Audio, and your microphone.

Click Interview Context to expand the context section and fill in:
  • Resume — paste your full resume text
  • Job Description — paste the JD for this role
  • Company Name
  • Interviewer LinkedIn (optional) — paste any info about your interviewer
  • Prep Notes (optional) — any notes from the recruiter
  • Culture Values (optional) — company values or culture notes
4
Start
Hit Start. HumanProx will verify your invite code and connect to your coach.
Open your interview meeting (Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, etc.) and start normally. HumanProx runs quietly in the background — your coach will see everything automatically.

That's it. Your coach will see your session and context automatically. Just focus on the interview.
